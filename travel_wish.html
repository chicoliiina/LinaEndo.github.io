Meet Cute Penguins!

Penguins have 18 species in the world in 2023. Let's meet a couple of them!

African Penguin

Also called Cape Penguin, it inhabits at a couple of locations alongside the African Coast.

Galapagos Penguin

The only penguin we can find north of the equator and it inhabits in the Galapagos Islands.

Emperor Penguin

The largest penguin in the world! It creates its colonies along the Antarctic Peninsula.
